### 📊 Product Evidence Evaluation
1. **User Efficiency**: What specific action does this sprint help the user perform faster or more efficiently?
2. **Step Reduction**: How many steps/clicks does this sprint eliminate for the user?
3. **Cognitive Load**: What information/effort does this sprint offload from the user's memory?
4. **KPI Impact**: Which primary KPI (efficiency, engagement, retention) is this sprint expected to improve, and by how much?
5. **Regression Risk**: What is the most critical path/subsystem at risk, and how have you verified it remains stable?
