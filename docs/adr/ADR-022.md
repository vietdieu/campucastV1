# ADR-022: Narrative Manifest v2

## Context
The previous `Briefing` and `Segment` models were too tied to the raw articles. We need a more flexible manifest format that can carry metadata for transitions, music cues, and specific voice directions.

## Decision
We introduce the **Narrative Manifest v2**.
1. **Beat-based structure**: The manifest mirrors the `NarrativeBeat` sequence.
2. **Rich Metadata**: Each beat carries `voice`, `speed`, `emotion`, and `backgroundMusic` instructions.
3. **Decoupled from Articles**: A beat can represent a transition or an intro that has no underlying `NewsItem`.

## Consequences
- The Runtime/Player can execute complex audio cues without knowing the narrative logic.
- Enables "Voice Director" (Sprint #014) to easily inject prosody instructions into the manifest.
- Future-proofs the audio pipeline for dynamic music and sound effect integration.
