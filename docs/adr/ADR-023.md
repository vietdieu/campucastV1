# ADR-023: Performance Director as a Broadcast Controller

## Context
The previous goal was "Voice Director". However, to achieve a professional AI Radio host experience (like Spotify AI DJ), we need more than just voice selection. We need control over prosody, emotion, pacing, and musical cues to create a "performance".

## Decision
We implement a **Performance Director Platform**.
1. **PerformanceProfile**: A consolidated domain object containing `VoiceProfile`, `ProsodyProfile`, `EmotionProfile`, `TimingProfile`, and `MusicProfile`.
2. **PerformanceDirector**: An engine that maps the `NarrativeBeat` intent to a specific `PerformanceProfile`.
3. **Performance Manifest Builder**: Extends the audio manifest to include detailed cues for prosody, music, and ducking.

## Consequences
- Elevates the AI DJ from "Reader" to "Performer".
- Provides granular control over audio aesthetics (`crossfade`, `gain`, `ducking`).
- Improves architectural modularity for future persona-based personas and styles.
