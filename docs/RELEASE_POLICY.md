# Release Policy

## Versioning Scheme
- **X.Y.0 (Architecture)**: Major structural or architectural updates.
- **X.Y.1 (Bug Fix)**: Critical fixes.
- **X.Y.2 (Performance)**: Performance optimization sprints.
- **3.2 (New Feature)**: Introduction of new user-facing features.
- **4.0 (Breaking Change)**: Major version updates with breaking changes.
